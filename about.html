About Us

Mega Spring Limited is an established Nursing and healthcare agency which provides, professional and reliable healthcare staff to our clients. Our vast experience gives our agency the edge in providing quality placement services to your organisation. providing staffing solutions ranging from registered nurses, carers, support workers, social workers, allied health care professionals and cleaners trained to a high standard. We specialise in providing skilled healthcare workers across the UK to clients requiring the best clinical staff on a temporary basis. Our company aims to ensure that exceptionally high standards of care are provided by our staff.

Mission

To Deliver safe, skilled, competent nurses, healthcare assistants to clients thereby providing our clients with person-centred approach and outstanding care. Our Agency staff always promote the health and wellbeing of each Client/Service User through a knowledgeable, open, honest, trustworthy, friendly, and helpful work ethic.

  • We follow the Care Quality Commission's rules, and we provide competitive commission rates.
  • Last-minute coverage.
  • Telephone service is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
  • We will match you with the most qualified healthcare practitioner available.
  • Ensuring that all of our nurses and caregivers look and plays the part.
  • Ongoing training courses ensure that all of our employees are proficient and are always attending refresher courses to match the industry's current requirements.
  • A very competitive fee when compared to other agencies.
