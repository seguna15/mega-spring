About Us

We understand that as an agency worker, sometimes it could be overwhelming going to different places, meeting new residents, learning new rules and it is difficult to create a bond with the people you care for, most times you want to stay in a place without changing. Mega Spring will offer you the opportunity to be posted to a place you want to be part of. If you like the idea of going places, then welcome aboard. We know that you do have choices and preferences and your opinion matter to us. Mega Spring regularly recruit for care positions across Bristol, Birmingham, Coventry and Hertfordshire. Staff choose the hours that suit them. We invest in our agency staff, providing access to more than 40 free training courses, as well as providing 24/7 support service to all our candidates should they require it. We also do our best to accommodate your needs as an individual with shift patterns. We Listen and we listen to you!

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN US

  • Weekly pay, with enhanced rates where applicable.
  • Building your own rota
  • Telephone service is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
  • Career enhancement.
  • Free Training
  • Fast and seamless recruiting process
